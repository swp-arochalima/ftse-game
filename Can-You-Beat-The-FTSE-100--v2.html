Can You Beat
The FTSE 100?

Is now a good time to buy? Are markets about to turn?

Tap Start and you'll be dropped into a random 10-year stretch of real FTSE 100 history, but you won't know which one. When big news breaks, you decide: sell and move to cash, buy back in, or hold your nerve. Try to beat a simple buy-and-hold investor who never touches their portfolio.

📊 Data: Real monthly FTSE 100 returns from April 2000 to May 2026, covering the UK's 100 largest listed companies, with dividends reinvested.

💰 Starting amount: £10,000.

📈 When invested: your pot rises and falls with the market each month.

🏦 When you sell: your cash earns 1% a year (a deliberately cautious assumption) while it sits on the sidelines.

🎯 Goal: beat the buy-and-hold investor over 10 years.
